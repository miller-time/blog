Hi, my name is Russell. I'm a Computer Science student at Portland State
University. This is a relatively new website I've created, and I'm not a web
developer or designer. This is mostly for me to have a place to write a personal
blog to write about my experiences with programming, school, work, and whatever
else I feel like writing about. I was just accepted to MECOP and will probably
have a lot to talk about when that starts this Spring.
I hope to be posting my resume somewhere on this site to refer employers to, but
for now I'll just say that I've been working remedial jobs for ten years now and
have been in and out of school. This is the most focus I have ever had and I'm
extremely excited about Computer Science and programming. I've done some work
with C, lots of "learning" with C++, some Java, some Haskell, a glimpse at Ruby,
and lately lots and lots of Python - my favorite so far.
