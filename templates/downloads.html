FAQ

Q: Do I really need all of these .dll files to run the Windows Qt programs?
A: Yes! However, you only need one copy of them. Just put all .exe files in the same folder!

Q: My OS isn't listed. What do I do?!
A: Download the source and compile it. Feel free to email me the binary! I have tried to compile all of my programs on the platforms that are available to me. That's the best I can do.
